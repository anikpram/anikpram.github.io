PhD Candidate at CBIG Lab


I am working towards a PhD in Computational Imaging from Electrical and Computer Engineering, The University of Iowa, Iowa City, IA, USA. My research focuses on developing novel computational techniques for solving inverse imaging problems. My recent works are related to accelerated Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) acquisition and recovery.
